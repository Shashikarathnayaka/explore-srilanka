Travel Tips

Everything you need to know before visiting Sri Lanka

Visa

Most visitors can obtain an ETA online. Valid for 30 days, extendable up to 6 months.

Best Time to Visit

West & south coast: December–March. East coast: May–September.

Currency

Sri Lankan Rupee (LKR). ATMs widely available. USD/EUR accepted at tourist sites.

Getting Around

Scenic train rides through the Hill Country are a must. Tuk-tuks for short distances.

Dress Code

Cover shoulders and knees when visiting temples.

Food

Try rice & curry, kottu roti, hoppers, and fresh seafood.
